# BrahMoID Hyperlocal Social vs My Panchayat App (GoI)

---

## 1. Purpose & Philosophy

| Aspect | My Panchayat App (GoI) | BrahMoID Hyperlocal Social |
|--------|------------------------|----------------------------|
| Core Intent | Information dissemination | Governance transformation |
| Nature | Departmental service app | Governance + accountability layer |
| Role | Displays panchayat data | Structures participation, oversight, and performance |
| Governance Model | Top-down | Bottom-up + participatory |

### Key Difference

My Panchayat shows what exists.  
BrahMoID measures how well governance works.
