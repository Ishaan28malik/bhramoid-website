# Our Funding Model

BrahMoID operates on transparent and ethical funding principles designed to preserve independence, neutrality, and public trust.

---

## Transparent Funding Principles

BrahMoID follows clear and ethical funding practices.

We do not:

- Accept donations in exchange for political favors  
- Engage in quid-pro-quo arrangements  
- Allow funding sources to influence governance tools  

Our funding comes from sources aligned with democratic values and transparency.

---

## Subscription-Based Platform

BrahMoID operates on a fair, transparent subscription model.

Political leaders and organisations pay for access to governance tools and services.

This model ensures:

- Financial sustainability  
- Platform accessibility  
- Independence from political influence  
- Neutrality in operations  

---

## Grants and Philanthropic Support

BrahMoID accepts grants from foundations and organisations that support:

- Democratic governance  
- Transparency  
- Citizen engagement  

### Safeguards

- All grants are publicly disclosed  
- Strict neutrality policies are maintained  
- Funding does not affect platform functionality or decisions  

---

## No Corporate Political Contributions

BrahMoID does not accept corporate contributions tied to political influence or favors.

We maintain a strict separation between:

- Funding sources  
- Political leaders using the platform  

This ensures governance tools remain independent and free from corporate capture.

---
