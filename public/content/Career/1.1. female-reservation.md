# Women Representation Policy  
### (Merit-First, Rule-Based)

BrahMoID recognises that women constitute nearly half of the grassroots electorate and play a decisive role in governance outcomes.

To ensure fair representation without compromising merit, BrahMoID applies a structured, transparent inclusion mechanism within its candidate selection framework.

---

## Core Principle

**Reservation guarantees inclusion. Merit determines priority.**

Women candidates are not selected *because* they are women.  
They are selected through the same examination, evaluation, and performance standards applied to all candidates.
